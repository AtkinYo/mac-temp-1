MACCABI MEMORABLE MOMENTS - Aimee Klooger
My Maccabi experience began in year 1 when my friends and I signed up to play basketball, although my career soon ended when mum and I realised that this was not the sport for me. Instead, I signed up for Maccabi netball. A memorable moment was my very first netball experience - I was so excited to be playing with some of my best friends! We had so much fun playing together and learning about each position but I’ll never forget running off the court crying because I didn’t have a player on me and didn’t know what to do! ﻿ Eventually, I figured it out and have loved coaching younger teams over the past few years to help them figure it out too. -@aimeeekloooger

Community Initiatives
Jeap App
JEAP is the Jewish Emergency Application giving you instant access to the two Victorian Jewish Community First Response groups.

At the push of a button, access CSG for security related incidents and Hatzolah for medical emergencies to obtain fastest response “when every second counts”.

We encourage all our members to download this App and hope that you never need to use it.

The Social Blueprint
The Social Blueprint is a comprehensive one-stop-shop for all our Melbourne Jewish community needs.

It’s a new hub that connects people to Jewish-focused organisations, services, businesses and events in Victoria.

The Social Blueprint aims to cater to all facets of our community without judgement and with open arms.

The Social Blueprint contains different aid resources as well as online events.
